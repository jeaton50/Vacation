Pack & Pro Tips

  • Pack layers: daytime mid‑80°F, evenings low‑60°F.
  • Bring hiking shoes and water‑friendly footwear.
  • Carry binoculars, refillable water bottles, and phone chargers.
  • Allow 15–30 min buffer per leg for breaks or traffic.
  • Check live navigation (Google Maps/Waze) before each departure.
